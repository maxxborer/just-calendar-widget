# Governance

Just Calendar Widget is maintained by [@maxxborer](https://github.com/maxxborer).

The maintainer makes final decisions on releases, roadmap, and contribution acceptance. Decisions prioritize a small native macOS experience, user privacy, accessibility, and long-term maintainability.

Contributors are welcome to discuss proposals in issues before investing in a large implementation. Maintainers will aim to acknowledge contributions promptly and explain declined changes respectfully.
